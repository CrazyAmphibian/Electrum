The Mestarialkemistipullo (lit. Master alchemist flask) operates similarly to a potion, though it has some notable differences
  1. Capacity - the Mestarialkemistipullo has 5x the capacity as a potion or 4x the capacity as a powder pouch.
  2. Materials - the Mestarialkemistipullo can capture liquids, as well as powders and gasses
  3. Obtaining - the Mestarialkemistipullo requires a short quest be done in order to gain it, it will never spawn naturally.
  4. Destructability - the Mestarialkemistipullo cannot be destroyed, and will respawn itself if it is. This includes midas and matter eater.
In order to obtain it, you must drop both a powder pouch and a potion onto the altar above the mountian. once done, the 2 items will be consumed, and a Mestarialkemistipullo containing the contents of both will be spawned just above the altar. This can only be done once per altar (once per [parallel] world per NG number).

The material quest

If a Mestarialkemistipullo is taken to the mountian altar, and it is filled with a single material that is alchemy related, the material will be consumed, and an alchemy-adjacent spell will be spawned. If the material has a droplet-style spell associated with it, then the droplet spell will be given instead. Every 5th time this is done, a more powerful alchemy spell will be given in addition.

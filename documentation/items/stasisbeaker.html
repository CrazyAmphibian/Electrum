The Stasis beaker is a sidegrade to the Mestarialkemistipullo. Its stats are identical (including indestructability) except for one key difference: the stasis beaker does not do alchemy reactions inside of it.

In order to obtain it, you must drop a Mestarialkemistipullo in the anvil inside the Hiisi base. The Mestarialkemistipullo will convert into a Statis beaker (this process retains any materials inside). This process is reversible by placing the stasis beaker onto the anvil.
